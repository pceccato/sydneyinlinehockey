Trading Post - For Sale, Wanted and Information Bulletins

This section of the site is for second hand gear/used gear- if you have any surplus hockey gear then please post it here. Getting fully kitted out for inline hockey can be expensive, so hopefully we can help out some newbies with kit by posting it here.

If you need any new gear like skates or wheels, please check out SkaterHQ at either their Manly or Moore Park stores. They have been supporting Monday Night Hockey since we started playing at Moore Park and will always look after you.
